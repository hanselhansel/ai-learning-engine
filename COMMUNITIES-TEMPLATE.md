# Community Engagement Tracker

## Strategy

Learning in public accelerates everything. This tracker follows a three-phase engagement model.

### Phase 1-2: Lurk Mode (Weeks 1-4)
- Join 8-10 communities across different platforms
- Read daily, observe patterns
- Note: who posts valuable content, what topics get traction, what questions get answered
- Do NOT post yet. Build context first.

### Phase 3: Narrow + Engage (Weeks 5-7)
- Pick 3-4 communities based on your SWOT and interests
- Engagement targets: 5-10 replies/week, 2-3 questions/week, 1 project share
- DM 2-3 interesting people

### Phase 4+: Contribute (Weeks 8+)
- Post original content (insights, analyses, project updates)
- Convert online connections to IRL meetings
- Track your content calendar and connection pipeline
